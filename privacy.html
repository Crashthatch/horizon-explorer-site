Privacy

Horizon Explorer complies with all UK / EU laws regarding privacy, and also complies with all Apple & Google policies for inclusion on their App Stores.
Data may be collected within these laws & policies to make the app work, to better understand our users and how they are using the app, to improve the service, to provide targeted advertisements, for analytics or for any other purpose.

Your data may be shared with other parties used to provide the Horizon Explorer service. eg. we must send your location to Wikipedia in order to find & display articles near you.

Horizon Explorer uses Mapbox to provide mapping data and user data is shared with them as detailed in their own Privacy Policy.
Specifically, Mapbox record opt-out anonymous background telemetry about users' locations & usage of the app.

Horizon Explorer uses Unity Analytics and Google Firebase Analytics to record what actions are being used within the app.

Horizon Explorer may use other third party services not specifically listed here.

By installing Horizon Explorer you consent to your data being used for any purpose within these laws & policies.
